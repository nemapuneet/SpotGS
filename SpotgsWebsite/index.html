HOW IT WORKS?

Augmented reality is a live direct or indirect view of a physical, real-world environment whose elements are augmented by computer-generated sensory input such as sound, video, graphics or GPS data. So, through AR our project aims to identify the buildings and important locations present in SGSITS college. Thus, we are aiming tto integrate AR with navigation
